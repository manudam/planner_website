How It Works

A study workflow designed to keep momentum from setup to exam day.

Planner Pig is not just a timetable generator. It helps you create a plan, turn it into work you can actually complete, and keep adjusting when real life gets in the way.

01

Build your exam plan

Add your exams, set dates, create topics, assign topic weightings, and define the study hours you realistically have each week.

  • Exam setup wizard
  • Topic breakdowns and timetables
  • Monthly planning view
02

Study with daily clarity

Switch from planning into action with Calendar Cat, List Lemur, and Progress Panther so every study session has structure.

  • Session-by-session calendar guidance
  • Task lists for each topic
  • Daily progress updates and rescheduling
03

Track momentum and stay accountable

Review what changed this week, monitor your study history, unlock richer progress stats, and share selected activity with buddies.

Feature Stories

Everything the app does, shown in the order students actually use it.

Planner Pig + List Lemur

Start with a realistic plan, not a blank page.

The planning engine helps you shape an exam schedule around available time, then List Lemur lets you break big topics into practical tasks such as reading, revision questions, or summary notes.

  • Create exams, topics, and timetable rules.
  • Adjust topic hours to fit the time you really have.
  • Turn planned study into milestone-based task lists.

Calendar Cat + Progress Panther

Know what to study today and what to do if you fall behind.

Calendar Cat shows planned sessions, while Progress Panther expands that into a daily action view with tasks, completion controls, and catch-up tools for unfinished work.

  • View upcoming sessions by exam and date.
  • Tick off completed tasks and log progress.
  • Reschedule outstanding study when plans change.

Overview Owl + History Horse + Bar Chart Bear

See your momentum without digging through your whole plan.

Planner Pig surfaces what matters now: what you studied recently, what is next, how many hours you are actually completing, and how your long-term progress is building over time.

  • Weekly overview of achievements and upcoming work.
  • History log for completed tasks, milestones, and Bear Tokens.
  • Progress charts that grow with your Studier Status.

Social Squirrel + Notifications + My Account

Stay motivated with reminders, buddy activity, and a profile that travels with you.

Planner Pig supports sign-in, profile setup, notification preferences, and a social layer for buddy requests, shared snapshots, and realtime motivation.

  • Buddy requests and profile views.
  • Realtime social feed with progress snapshots.
  • Regular, follow-up, and buddy notification settings.

FAQ

Quick answers before launch.

What can I do in Planner Pig?

You can plan exams, build topic structures, generate study schedules, create task lists, update daily progress, review charts and history, configure reminders, and share selected progress with buddies.

Is the app just for mobile?

No. The launch website is designed around support for iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac, and Web so the core experience can travel with you.

How many exams can I set up?

Planner Pig supports planning multiple exams, each with its own topics, timetable rules, and progress history.

Will I be able to adjust my plan after I start?

Yes. Planner Pig is built for ongoing updates, including timetable changes, topic changes, rescheduling, and catch-up actions when your study pattern shifts.
