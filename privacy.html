Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TriangleCarrot collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use Planner Pig and any other website, mobile app, or software product that links to this page.

Last updated: April 26, 2026. This page is designed to act as the default privacy policy for TriangleCarrot apps unless a product includes a more specific policy.

1. Scope

This policy applies to Planner Pig and other TriangleCarrot products that link to it. If a specific app, site, or feature provides a separate privacy notice, that more specific notice will apply to that service instead.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on the product and features you use, we may collect:

  • Account details such as your name, email address, sign-in credentials, and profile preferences.
  • Content you create or upload, including plans, schedules, tasks, progress updates, reminders, templates, and other in-app content.
  • Social or sharing data if you use community features, such as buddy requests, profile details, and activity you choose to share with other users.
  • Support communications you send to us, including emails, questions, bug reports, and feedback.
  • Technical and usage information such as device type, browser type, app version, approximate location derived from IP address, crash data, and diagnostic logs.
  • Notification-related data, such as device tokens or preferences, when you enable reminders or alerts.

3. How We Use Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and secure our apps, websites, and services.
  • Create and manage user accounts, sync data across devices, and personalize the user experience.
  • Send service messages, reminders, support replies, and other essential product communications.
  • Improve features, troubleshoot issues, analyze performance, and develop new functionality.
  • Enforce our terms, prevent abuse, detect fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our websites and apps may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, improve performance, and support security and core functionality.

5. When We Share Information

We may share information in the following situations:

  • With service providers that help us host, support, deliver, secure, or improve our services.
  • With other users when you intentionally use social, sharing, or profile-based features.
  • When required to comply with law, regulation, legal process, or valid government request.
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of all or part of our business.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain legitimate business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations. Retention periods can vary depending on the type of data and the product feature involved.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Subject to your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request a copy of the information you provided to us.

You can also manage some information directly inside the app, such as profile settings, notification preferences, or content you choose to remove.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

Our products are not intended to collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us without appropriate permission, please contact us so we can review and remove it where required.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than the one where you live. Where required, we take appropriate steps to protect personal information transferred across borders.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the last updated date above.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to make a privacy request, contact TriangleCarrot at plannerpig@trianglecarrot.com.
